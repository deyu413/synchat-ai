Política de Privacidad

Última actualización: 2025-05-23

Introducción

SynChat AI ("nosotros", "nuestro") se compromete a proteger tu privacidad. Esta Política de Privacidad explica cómo recopilamos, usamos, divulgamos y protegemos tu información cuando utilizas nuestro sitio web y nuestros servicios (el "Servicio").

Información que Recopilamos

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, and information automatically collected when you use our services, such as IP address and usage data.

Cómo Usamos Tu Información

We use your information to provide and improve our services, communicate with you, process payments, and for other business purposes.

Cómo Compartimos Tu Información

We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate our services, or as required by law.

Seguridad de los Datos

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Tus Derechos de Protección de Datos (GDPR/LOPD)

You may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to help provide and improve our services. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Contacto

Si tienes preguntas sobre esta Política de Privacidad, contacta con nosotros en privacy@synchatai.com.
